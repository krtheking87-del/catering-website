✦ end credits ✦
to: her · from: me · re: everything

There is this thing that happens when she walks into a room. It is not loud. Nobody announces it. But the air shifts slightly — warmer, somehow — and I notice it every single time, even after all this time.

I think about the way she smells — not perfume exactly, just her, whatever that is — and how it is the most comforting thing I have ever known. I think about her eyes when she smiles. They do this thing where they go first, just a half-second before the rest of her face catches up. I live for that half-second.

She has these little nicknames for me and she uses them like they are nothing, like they did not quietly rearrange something in my chest the first time she said them. She makes me feel understood — not in the way people say they understand you, but in the way that means she actually listens. She actually sees me.

And the way she eats food she loves. I cannot explain it. She just gets this look — fully present, no performance, just honest joy — and I think it is one of the most beautiful things I have ever seen in my entire life. She does not even know she does it.

I do not know what I did to deserve any of this. I just know I am not letting go.

— yours, on every frame of film 🎞️
