Why I love her — completely

♡
To her · From me · Re: everything, always

I did not plan to build this. I kept thinking nothing I had — no text, no card, no word spoken out loud — was quite big enough. So I made a page. A whole page, just for you, that will live on the internet saying the same thing every time you visit: you are loved. Completely. Without condition. Without expiry.

You are the kind of person the world does not produce very often. The kind who smells like home and looks at me like I am something worth looking at. The kind whose eyes smile a half-second before her face catches up — and I have been watching for that half-second since the first time I saw it happen. I still catch my breath.

I love you for the obvious reasons and for the small secret ones — the nicknames you use like they cost nothing, the way you eat food you love with pure honest joy, the fact that you actually listen. Not politely. Actually. I have never felt more seen in my life.

So here is this page. Ridiculous and earnest and completely yours. Come back to it whenever you need reminding. I will keep saying it: you are my favourite person. Full stop. No asterisk. No fine print. Just you.

— yours, completely & always 🌸
